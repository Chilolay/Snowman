1. Type ( before word to get beginning Christmas swashes and Type ) after word to get the ending Christmas swashes.
2. Type < (less sign) before word to get beginning standard swashes and Type > (greater sign) after word to get the ending Standard swashes.
   Example : (Christmas Bell) or <Christmas Bell>
